IFRS 9 Financial Instruments

Understanding Credit Risk Migration

IFRS 9 requires financial institutions to recognize expected credit losses based on forward-looking information and significant increases in credit risk (SICR).

The Three Stages of IFRS 9

Stage 1

Performing Assets

  • No significant increase in credit risk since initial recognition
  • 12-month expected credit losses recognized
  • Interest revenue on gross carrying amount

Default Probability

Stage 2

Underperforming (SICR)

  • Significant increase in credit risk detected
  • Lifetime expected credit losses recognized
  • Interest revenue on gross carrying amount

Default Probability

Stage 3

Credit-Impaired

  • Objective evidence of impairment
  • Lifetime expected credit losses recognized
  • Interest on amortized cost (net of provision)

Default Probability
